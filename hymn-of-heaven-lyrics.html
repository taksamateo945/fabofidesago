About

Phil Wickham is an American Christian musician, singer, and songwriter. He was born on April 5, 1984, in San Diego, California, and grew up in a musical family. Wickham began leading worship at his youth group when he was just 13 years old. He released his first album, “Give You My World,” in 2003, which was followed by several more albums over the years.

Wickham’s music is known for its uplifting and inspiring lyrics, as well as its catchy melodies. Some of his most popular songs include “This Is Amazing Grace,” “Living Hope,” and “At Your Name (Yahweh, Yahweh).” Wickham has won multiple awards for his music, including several Dove Awards.

In addition to his music career, Wickham is also involved in various charitable organizations and ministries. He currently resides in Orange County, California, with his wife and three children.
