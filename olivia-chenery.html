Career and Professional Life

Olivia Chenery’s first acting role was in the 2008 short film “Flushed”. And, then she worked in “Penguin” in the year 2011. It is a short film. Likewise, in the year 2015, she also starred in a comedy-drama movie “Legacy”. She also appeared in 10×10, Playing Dead, Silent Witness, and Endeavour. She got additional fame after playing Scarlett ‘Scooch’ Hailton in Season 2 of the TV series Bulletproof in the year 2020.
