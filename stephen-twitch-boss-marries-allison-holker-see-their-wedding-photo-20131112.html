Stephen &quot;tWitch&quot; Boss Marries Allison Holker: Wedding Photos

August 2024 · 2 minute read

Introducing Mr. and Mrs. Boss! So You Think You Can Dance all-stars Stephen “tWitch” Boss and Allison Holker said “I do” on Tuesday, Dec. 10, in a rustic chic ceremony at Nigel Lythgoe‘s Villa San Juliette Vineyard and Winery in Paso Robles, Calif. — and from the looks of these wedding photos, the day was a perfect 10.

Holker, a top 10 finalist on season two of SYTYCD, looked gorgeous in a strapless Watters gown of ivory lace and silk organza, with embroidered, beaded leaves and flowers on the bodice and train. Her veil, from Watters by Toni Federici, featured crystals and a hand-cut edge, and she wore peep-toe jewel-encrusted shoes by Badgley Mischka. The finishing touch? White gold and diamond leaf earrings from Jay Carlile & Co.

Boss, meanwhile, was dapper in a black tuxedo and plum polka-dot bow tie, which matched the plum crinkle chiffon bridesmaids dresses. Holker’s wedding party included 10 people, including three fellow SYTYCD alum: Courtney Galiano, Kathryn McCormick, and Travis Wall (one of two “bridesmen”). She also had two flower girls: her 5-year-old daughter, Weslie Renae Fowler, and Boss’ 8-year-old cousin, Alayna Boss.

On Boss’ side, there were nine groomsmen and a bridesmaid, three of whom — William Wingfield, Joshua Allen, and Comfort Fedoke — competed on SYTYCD.

After the ceremony, guests including Dancing With the Stars pros Anna Trebunskaya and Chelsie Hightower were treated to a feast of coconut beer shrimp, artichoke and goat cheese fritters, grilled eggplant and basil pizzas, prime tenderloin skillet steak, shrimp-crusted wild salmon, and wild mushroom ravioli. Champagne cake with vanilla mousse finished the meal.

The couple also surprised their loved ones with a choreographed routine to Justin Bieber’s “Somebody to Love.” Their first dance, though, was to Adele’s “One & Only.” 

“Thank you all so much for all your love on the Boss Wedding 2013,” Holker tweeted the next morning. “It was a day full of love and happiness. JUST PERFECT.”
