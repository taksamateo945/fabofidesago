Gisele Bundchen, Tom Brady Hang With Bridget Moynahan: Picture

February 2024 · 2 minute read

This family gets an A for amicable! Gisele Bundchen and husband Tom Brady enjoyed a day at the park in NYC on Sunday, May 18, with the quarterback’s ex Bridget Moynahan. The trio greeted each other with hugs, before Gisele and Tom took a seat on the grass beside Moynahan as they watched a little league game.

Gisele Bundchen’s Style Evolution 

Bundchen, 33, went casual in jeans, knee-high boots, and a loose white sweatshirt, while her husband, 36, wore a stone-gray top over a white collared shirt. Shielding her face with a panama hat, Blue Bloods actress Moynahan, 44, opted for a chambray shirt and black jeans.

Celebrities Who Love Models 

Exes Brady and Moynahan, who share 6-year-old son Jack, appeared to comfortable with each other during the outing—bolstering Moynahan’s comments from years ago that she will never speak poorly about her ex or his wife. “I never made a comment about Gisele or Tom publicly,” Moynahan told More magazine in its March 2011 issue. “I have a relationship with these people on a daily basis. I’m raising a child, and it’s public. My son has two loving parents and an extended family, whether it’s cousins or stepmothers or boyfriends. My son is surrounded by love.”
