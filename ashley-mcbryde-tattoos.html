Ashley McBryde Bio & Family

The singer was born on 29 July 1983 in Waldron, Arkansas, to parents, William C. McBryde and Martha A. Wilkins. Ashley went to her nearby high-school near her hometown and then the Arkansas State University.

The county singer stands at the height of 5 feet 3 inches (1.6 m) tall and weighs around 62 kg.

Ashley and Her Tattoo Fascination

Apart from music, Ashley’s fascination is tattoos. Her favorite tattoo artist is Nancy Miller. She trusts Miller and mostly prefers to visit her at Main Street Tattoo. Nancy is responsible for creating and designing the beautiful artwork, including sunglasses-and-lipstick ink in Ashley’s song American Scandal. She prefers traditional tattoo and feels the tramp stamp is the ugliest ink in her body.

Career & Net Worth

The 36-year-old net worth must touch a million-dollar figure from a professional music career since 2006. Some of her chartbusters songs include One Night Standards (2019), Radioland (2018), and A Little Dive Bar in Dahlonega (2017), to name a few.

After her second album release, she got the fortune to be on tour across the Southern United States. Her 2017 song, A Little Dive Bar in Dahlonega got featured as one of the 54 best musical creations of the year named by The New York Times. A year later, she released her debut album Girl Going Nowhere, produced by Jay Joyce, that got released on 30 March 2018.
